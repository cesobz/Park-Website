History

Located in a desert oasis just outside of San Diego, Bucklin Park has been a home to many events since it first opened in the 1950s. Sitting on over 20 acres of land, this park includes; spacious areas for picnnics and recreational activities, a pond that is home to "Famous Ducks", a playground area for kids to enjoy and overall provides a nice view for many to come enjoy. While Bucklin Park has changed many things overtime, one thing that will never change is the fun memories that are to be made here with friends and family.




Gallery

Park Plateau

Located in the center of the park, this area most commonly host any event or recreational sport. The running path also cuts through this area allowing everyone the opportunity to enjoy this wonderous view

Children's Playground

Position on the south side of the park, this is a popular area for children of all ages. Benches and water fountains surround the borders of this playground and provide plenty of shade for those looking to take a break from all the fun

Water Fountain

Located on the east side of the park, this water fountain provides a stunning view. Seating is spaced out to allow for a more calming atomsphere that allows individuals to close their eyes and just take it all in.

Famous Ducks

Situated just north of the main Park Plateau, is the large pond that is home to what the locals have dubbed, the Famous Ducks! Those who have been coming here for years all have funny stories about being chased by one of these guys. Please respect the signs surounding this area.
